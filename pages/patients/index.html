Patient Information

Inservio acts as the agent for the patient arranging the medication to be dispensed by a pharmacy and delivered to your clinic in time for your appointment. Our service ensures that you do not need to collect your medication from a local pharmacy, and you do not have to worry about transporting the refrigerated medications to your appointment. We maintain product quality by managing and monitoring the whole process prior to injection.
